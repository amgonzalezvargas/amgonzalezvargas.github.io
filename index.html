About Me

Currently, I'm mostly working in applications of data science and AI in health care and education. I have a diverse background, with degrees in computer science, biomedical engineering, mechatronics engineering, hospital technology management, and higher education.

I have worked in academy for more than 15 years, designing and teaching courses in machine learning, biomedical instrumentation, computational biology, and health information systems. My research career has been carried out at various institutions, including Universidad Autónoma de Occidente, Universidad Santiago de Cali, Institut National de Recherche en Informatique et en Automatique (INRIA), University of Pavia and ETH Zürich.

I've supervised many theses and authored or co-authored over 30 peer-reviewed publications. In 2021 I was honored with the 'The Night of the Best 2021' Award in the category 'Professors driving the transformation of education mediated with digital technologies' by the Ministry of National Education in Colombia, and currently I'm an Associate Investigator in the Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation's classification system.

I'm always looking for new challenges and fantastic things to learn.
